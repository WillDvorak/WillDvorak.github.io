Fantastic Mr. Fox

This movie shows a story of a fox who lives in a cave with his family but dreams for more, so elaborate schemes are concocted and friends are made.

Why It's my favorite

  • The stop motion animation
  • The funny and emotional characters
  • George Clooney as Mr. Fox
  • I have a soft spot for Wes Anderson films
