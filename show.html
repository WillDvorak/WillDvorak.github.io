One Piece

This show is about a overconfident young lad who believes that he can become the king of the pirates, though time and time again he is forced to prove himself worthy of the title.

My favorite parts

  1. The character development
  2. The length of the show (1170+ episodes and multiple movies)
  3. The power system and scaling
  4. Devil fruits are a very interesting way to get powers
    • their drawback is interesting for a world covered in water

I would recommend this show to someone who enjoys worldbuilding and action.
