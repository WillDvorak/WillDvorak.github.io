Deadlock

Deadlock is the most fun game I've ever played and its not even out yet. It perfectly mixes movement and action with strategy and teamwork

I think about playing this game far more than I should, and when I play it hours can pass without me realizing until its too late.

I can honestly see myself playing this game for the next few years as they continue to add more to it.
